How to Play

  • You have 60 seconds to triage as many security alerts as possible
  • For each alert, choose to Ignore, Escalate, or Mitigate
  • Your choices affect 4 meters: Noise, Damage, Risk, and Career
  • If any meter drops to -10, it's game over
  • Keep your Career meter high and the rest low to maximize your score
